Meet Irae
Originally hailing from the beautiful islands of American Samoa, Irae Pagofie now resides and performs music in the Seattle area. For over 20 years, Irae has been performing a diverse set of music styles and genres. From rhythm and blues to reggae island music, you will be pleased no matter what you hear. Irae enjoys playing for live events, such as weddings or other gatherings. Call Irae today for booking availability information.
